Admission process

Simple steps to secure your place.

We believe the application journey should feel clear and supportive. Families are guided through every step, from inquiry to enrollment.

1

Submit inquiry

Fill out the online form or email our admissions team to request information.

2

Campus tour

Visit our campus, meet teachers, and feel the BrightFuture community firsthand.

3

Enrollment review

Share key student details so we can match your child to the right class and support plan.

4

Start school

Receive a welcome packet and begin with a smooth transition into our vibrant learning community.

Admission requirements

What we need

  • Completed application form
  • Previous school records or a teacher reference
  • Family interview or visit
  • Optional learning portfolio

Support available

Our team can help with guidance on deadlines, special needs support, and transition planning for new students.
